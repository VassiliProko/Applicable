# Applicable — Vision

> "Talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. We exist to close that gap."

## Mission

To create a credible and accessible system that enables self-taught individuals to prove real-world skills without relying on expensive traditional degrees.

## Core Goals

1. **Bridge the verification gap** between learning and employment
2. **Provide trusted, tangible proof** of skills for employers
3. **Enable economic mobility** for lower-income, self-driven learners

## Why This Exists

- Traditional education is becoming financially inaccessible
- Learning is now widely available online, but credibility is not
- Millions of motivated individuals are undervalued due to lack of proof, not lack of ability

## Stakeholders (prioritized)

1. **Primary Users (Gen Z learners)** — We are empowering, fair, and opportunity-driven
2. **Employers** — We are reliable, credible, and a signal of real skill
3. **Education/EdTech Platforms** — We are complementary, not disruptive

## Competitive Advantage

- Focus on **verification**, not just learning
- Combines skill-building + proof + credibility in one system
- Designed specifically for self-taught, resource-constrained learners

## Competition

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness |
|---|---|---|
| Traditional Universities | Strong credibility | Low accessibility |
| Online Platforms (Coursera, Udemy) | High access | Low verification |
| Bootcamps | Structured learning | Expensive |

## Values & Beliefs

- **Merit over credentials** — what you can do matters more than where you learned it
- **Accessibility & equity** — no one should be locked out by cost
- **Transparency & accountability** — our verification means something because it's rigorous
- **Real-world impact over theory** — skills that translate to jobs, not just certificates

## Key Risks

- Gaining employer trust
- Competing with established institutions
- Ensuring quality and credibility at scale
- Avoiding becoming another platform that teaches but doesn't validate

## Vision Timeline

- **5 Years:** Recognized platform for skill verification and portfolios
- **10 Years:** A widely accepted alternative credential system trusted by employers

## Success Metrics

- Number of users who gain employment or income growth
- Employer trust and adoption of the system
- Demonstrated skill-to-job conversion rate

## Marketing Direction

- Leverage Gen Z channels (TikTok, YouTube, online communities)
- Share success stories & proof-based outcomes
- Position as a "degree alternative" focused on results

## Industry Trends

- Rise of AI-assisted learning
- Shift toward skills-based hiring
- Increasing skepticism around traditional degrees
- Growth of self-directed, online education
